$var title: Mosses and Ferns

This sage study grants knowledge of mosses and ferns.

Abilities

Amateur (10-29 points)

  • Identify Mosses and Ferns
  • Cultivate Mosses and Ferns
  • Protective Bracken
  • Sylvan Bedstead

Authority (30-59 points)

  • Vermifuge (expel parasites from hosts)
  • Staunch Wounds (bind wounds using mosses, equal to or better than using bandages)
  • Create Terrarium (create a living mini ecosystem with a calming effect)
  • Moss Lawn (cultivate large areas of moss for a refined aesthetic effect, improving the value of gardens and other green spaces; possibly getting you jobs from noblemen, landowners, etc.)
  • Harvest Peat (for special potions, or making whiskey)

Expert and Sage (60-99 points)

TBD. Possibilities:

  • Contemplative Retreat (craft a superior isolated site for druids to meditate, raising power of certain spells)
  • Moss Walls and Roofs (build structures such that mosses grow on them, offering magical protections, especially against precipitation)
  • Hybridization (create new mosses and ferns)
  • Outcrossing (create moss/fern monsters)
