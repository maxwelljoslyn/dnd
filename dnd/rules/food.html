$var title: Food

Characters must consume food proportionate to the amount of effort they expend on a given day. The required amount can be broken up into meals or snacks as the character sees fit.

Required Consumption

  • A character who performs little activity, such as when resting to heal HP, must eat 1 pound of food that day.
  • A character who performs moderate activity, including shopping at the market or traveling, must eat 2 pounds of food that day.
  • A character who participates in combat must eat 3 pounds of food that day.

Beverages

All forms of milk, including kumiss, are highly nutritious, and count as 0.75 pound of food per pint.

Alcoholic beverages besides kumiss count as 0.25 pound of food per pint.

Using Food Before It Goes Bad

Some foods can go bad very quickly, while others keep for a long time. See Degradation for more about how well food keeps.
