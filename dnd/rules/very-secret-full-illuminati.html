$var title: Illuminati

This rare and powerful sage study is available only to those who have been inducted into the Illuminati. Little is known about this organization save by those who are part of it.

Novice Members

Player characters with a 17-19 Wisdom have a 5-15% chance to begin gameplay as a novice member of the Illuminati. A novice knows one or two basic secret signs by which to recognize other Illuminati. They may attend Councils by invitation only, and then without voting privileges.

Such a character will gain d4-1 points per level in this study.

Abilities

Amateur (10-29 points)

  • Secret Master: become a fully-initiated member of the Illuminati, with the right to freely attend and vote in any Council led by the Secret Princes.
  • Signs and Grips: learn code phrases, signs, and handshakes by which Illuminati recognize one another.
  • Seek Agents: request access to special hirelings sworn to serve another individual Illuminatus.

Authority (30-59 points)

  • Secret Knight: attain a higher rank, with the right to petition a Council, and to grow the ranks of the Illuminati and their human tools.
  • Recruit Agents: gain the sworn allegiance of people with skills useful to furthering the goals of the Illuminati.
  • Induct Illuminatus: select a promising candidate, and offer them admittance to the Illuminati as a novice member. They may or may not accept. Some Councils will insist on confirming the candidate's suitability before you can make them an offer.

Expert (61-99 points)

  • Secret Prince: attain a higher rank, with the possibility of controlling a Council, or creating a new one.
  • Create Secret Master: perform the rite which turns a novice into a fully-initiated member of the Illuminati.
  • Recruit Worthy Agents: gain the sworn allegiance of especially skilled and powerful people, such as wealthy squires, famed craftsmen, or minor noblemen.

  • Secret Lord: attain the highest rank of the Illuminati, with the possibility of
  • Invoke Council
  • Ascend to Grand Council
  • Manipulate Politics
