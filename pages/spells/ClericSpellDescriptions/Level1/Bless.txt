Bless
Range: 60 feet
Casting Time: 1 round
Duration: 1 hour
Area of Effect: 30 foot radius circle
Reversible: yes
Save: none
Type: Enchantment

Bless raises morale by +1 and also raises attack rolls by +1. The recipients do not need to be believers, as it is the cleric's will that they be bolstered.

The reverse form causes the opposite of both effects.
