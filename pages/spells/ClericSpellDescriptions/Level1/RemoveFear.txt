Remove Fear
Range: 15 feet
Casting Time: 1 round
Duration: 1 minute; special
Area of Effect: 1 creature
Reversible: yes
Save: Magic if reversed
Type: Enchantment

Remove Fear raises the target creature's saving throw against fear (including magical effects) by +4 for one minute.

If the target has already been affected by fear, then the spell grants them another saving throw against the effect, with a +1 bonus for each level of the caster.

The reversed spell causes the victim to flee at maximum movement away from the caster for 1 round per caster level.
